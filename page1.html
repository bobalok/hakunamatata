Briefing

Who we are?

Hakuna Matata Asia Pte Ltd is operating worldwide via an extensive international network of agents and offices in over Asian countries.

Principal

Our principal activities are Supply, Engineering, Procurement & Construction (EPC), Project Management and ongoing support of advanced technology Fire, Safety, Cyber Security and Defence solutions to governments and government law enforcement agencies, non-governmental organizations and blue chip commercial organizations worldwide

Experience

We have extensive experience gained over many years in the EPC of a wide range of security and defence solutions including a wide range of anti-terrorism equipment & systems, border security systems and homeland security solutions.

Natural Resources

Our Oil and Gas division is engaged in supply of the materials and equipment for overseas projects.

Market Value

Hakuna Matata consistently insists on the development policy of “high quality, good benefits and sustainability”, strives to strengthen the efforts on international market exploration
